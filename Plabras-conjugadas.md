# PASADO

## pasado - verbos regular (-ed)

**verbo + ed** \

* start - started
* play - played
* clean - cleaned
* finish - finished
* listen - listened
* prepare - prepared
* dance - danced
* try - tried

## pasado - verbos irregular

**no hay regla** \

* buy - bought
* spoke - speak
* eat - ate
* have - had
* go - went
* take - took
* wake - woke
* drive - drove
* swim - swam
